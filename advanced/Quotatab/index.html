ProFTPD Developer's Guide: mod_quotatab

ProFTPD Version 1.2

Table of Contents

The mod_quotatab module for ProFTPD is used to provide quotas on users in a flexible and extensible manner. In addition to its main duty of tracking usage, mod_quotatab can be extended to use other storage formats for its quota table information by writing submodules. It also makes available its quota information to other interested "consumers" of its data.
