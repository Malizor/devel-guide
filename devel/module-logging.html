Debugging
When developing a module, please make use of log_debug() where appropriate. Not only will this aid you as your module develops, but it will help users report potential problems once your module is released. It is also highly recommended to #define a name for your module, like so:

  • proftpd -n -d 5 for debugging
  • log_debug() for debugging statements Signal 11 == usually, a NULL pointer: 
        localhost.localdomain (localhost.localdomain[127.0.0.1]) - ProFTPD terminating (signal 11)
